Elder Baginski's Mission

Home, Resume, Cool Facts

I served my mission from 2019-2021 in the beautiful state of Florida. My Mission was the Florida Fort Lauderdale Mission, and I spent about a year and a half serving in the greater city of Miami. It was quite the experience! I was a Spanish-speaking missionary and had the chance to teach and baptize families from almost every country south of the US, excluding Brazil. I taught a lot of people who fled their nations because of financial difficulty. Hit this link to go to see more about this, its an interesting subject!
