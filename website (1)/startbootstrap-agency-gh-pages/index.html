Rocks

"Build your house upon a rock" said Jesus, or maybe Emma?

You could have sworn she was born with a rock in her hand. Emma has been fascinated with rocks since at least the Paleozoic Era. She knows more about rocks than probably anything else. Just don't ask her any specific questions, she'll probably tell you it is limestone.

Musicals

I can't tell if I love it or I hate it, but she loves them.

Whether it is studying for a test or driving from New York to California, Emma will be sure to have these classics playing from start to finsh all the way to your final desination.

Taylor Swift

I don't think Emma would let me not know when a new Swift album drops.

No matter how much Emma loves Musicals, she will always be sure to play some taylor swift. In the time I have been with Emma, I think there hasn't been a day where something Taylor Swift was not playing in the background. If I am wrong about that, then those must have been her grumpy days.

Tika

This one really surprised me when we first started dating.

When we started dating, everything seemed normal until out of nowhere Emma was wearing a shirt with a dog covered in a sweater. I of course asked her and turns out Emma buys merchandise for an online dog model named Tika. At first I thought it was just this dog, but later I found out Emma likes all animals that are tiny and have no fur. Case and point, Emma now wants a hairless cat...

Cuddles

I am not a cuddly person, but Emma is... AND I LOVE THAT!

There isn't much to say here. Emma is a cuddler at heart. If we are ever doing anything, there is a good chance Emma needs a cuddle. I havent figured out if it is for warmth or some other practical reason yet. I will keep you posted if I find out.

Panera Bread

The blood coursing through Emma's veins has got to be 10% water and 90% Panera mac-n-cheese.

On our first roadtrip to California Emma asked if we could specifically stop at Panera. At first this was not that crazy. Now, everytime we go to california or any other state with Panera, we have to stop. Turns out, this is her holy water. She just needs it to live.
